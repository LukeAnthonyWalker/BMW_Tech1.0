About the vehicles.

The mobility demands of tomorrow in the BMW models of today: thanks to state-of-the-art technology and powerful lithium-ion high-performance batteries, the eDrive inspire with a constant, tremendous torque and sustainable efficiency. They thus combine future-oriented mobility with tremendous driving fun. A powerful BMW TwinPower Turbo engine and a highly efficient electric motor propel the BMW X5 xDrive40e while the BMW xDrive all-wheel drive system guarantees best-possible roadholding and outstanding agility The BMW 7 Series with iPerformance is the luxurious solution for tomorrow’s sustainable mobility. In the BMW 330e, the combination of electric motor and BMW TwinPower Turbo engine brings together the best of both worlds.

About BMW E-Drive

A completely new dimension of driving pleasure: the drive concept BMW eDrive boasts impressive dynamics and, when driven in purely electrical mode, is emission-free locally. The newly-developed synchronous electric motor dazzles with enormous torque that remains constant from acceleration to maximum speed – for sustainable mobility with overwhelming agility.

LITHIUM-ION HIGH-PERFORMANCE BATTERY.

All vehicles with BMW eDrive technology derive energy for electrical driving from a sensational high-tech electrical battery. The powerful lithium-ion high-performance batteries are combines with a cooling system that keeps the battery at an optimal operating temperature and thus increases performance and life expectancy.

ELECTRIC MOTOR.

The electric motor in the BMW eDrive concept is a proprietary development by the BMW Group and an integral part of all electric-powered BMW models.

BMW ActiveE.

The first local emissions-free vehicle with BMW eDrive technology that’s produced on a small scale for a pilot project. Findings and experience gathered from the BMW ActiveE were applied in the development of the BMW i3.
